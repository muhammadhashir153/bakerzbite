Croissants

Plain Croissant

Price: 150 pkr / 6 pcs

Ingredients

  1. Flour: All-purpose flour or bread flour is commonly used.
  2. Butter: High-quality unsalted butter is essential for the flaky layers.
  3. Water: Cold water is used to hydrate the dough.
  4. Milk: Some recipes may include milk for added richness and flavor.
  5. Sugar: A small amount of sugar may be added for a touch of sweetness.
  6. Salt: Salt is used to enhance the flavor of the croissant dough.
  7. Yeast: Active dry yeast or instant yeast is used as a leavening agent.
  8. Egg: A beaten egg may be used for glazing the croissants before baking.

Chocolate Croissant

Price: 180 pkr / 6 pcs

Ingredients

  1. Dark Chocolate: High-quality dark chocolate bars or chocolate batons are traditionally used. The chocolate is typically cut into thin sticks or batons that are placed inside the croissant.
  2. Egg Wash: A beaten egg mixed with a little milk or water is used to give the croissant a shiny and golden crust.
  3. Powdered Sugar: It can be sprinkled on top of the baked croissants for decoration.
  4. Vanilla Extract: A small amount of vanilla extract can be added to the dough for extra flavor.

Almond Croissant

Price: 220 pkr / 6 pcs

Ingredients

  1. Croissant Dough: Use the same ingredients as mentioned earlier for the plain croissant dough.
  2. Almond Filling:
    • Almond Flour: Finely ground blanched almonds or almond flour.
    • Butter: Unsalted butter, softened.
    • Powdered Sugar: Adds sweetness to the filling.
    • Almond Extract: A small amount of almond extract enhances the almond flavor.
    • Egg: A beaten egg is used to bind the filling together.
  3. Sliced Almonds: Used to sprinkle on top of the croissants for added crunch and visual appeal.
  4. Powdered Sugar: Can be dusted on top of the baked croissants for decoration.

Ham and Cheese Croissant

Price: 300 pkr / 6 pcs

Ingredients

  1. Croissant Dough: Use the same ingredients as mentioned earlier for the plain croissant dough.
  2. Ham: Thinly sliced ham, such as cooked ham or deli ham.
  3. Cheese: A variety of cheese can be used, such as Swiss, cheddar, or Gruyere. Choose a cheese that melts well and complements the ham.
  4. Dijon Mustard (optional): Dijon mustard can be spread on the croissant for an extra tangy flavor.
  5. Egg Wash: A beaten egg mixed with a little milk or water is used to brush on the croissant before baking.
